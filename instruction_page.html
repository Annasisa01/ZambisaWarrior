Instructions on how to play

  • Press the up arrow key jump
  • Press the left arrow key to move left
  • Press the right arrow key to move right
  • Press the down arrow key to crouch or slide while running
  • Press the A key for light attack
  • Press the S key for heavy attack
  • Press the A + A + A keys for combo attacks
  • Press the A + S + keys for special attack 1
  • Press the A + S + keys for special attack 2
  • Use the SPACE key to pause/play the game
